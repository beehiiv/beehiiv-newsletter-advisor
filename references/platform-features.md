## Content & Editor

### Post Editor
- Rich-text drag-and-drop editor
- Newsletter Builder 2.0: multiple templates per publication
- Bulk Actions for Posts: manage content library at scale (archive, delete, tag in bulk)
- Add images via drag/drop, upload, library, or URL
- Custom fonts (Google Fonts, Adobe Fonts, or upload your own)
- Feature page: https://www.beehiiv.com/features/editor

### Customization
- Custom branding: logo, colors, domain
- Newsletter templates library
- Per-publication template defaults
- Feature page: https://www.beehiiv.com/features/customization

### Polls
- Embed polls directly into emails
- Boosts engagement and sender reputation with ISPs
- Poll responses feed into segmentation
- Feature page: https://www.beehiiv.com/features/polls

### Audio / Podcasts
- Import existing podcast or start new
- Auto-sync new episodes
- Host podcast website alongside newsletter and digital products
- Feature page: https://www.beehiiv.com/features/audio-newsletter

### Dynamic Content ⭐ (Most requested feature of 2025)
- Personalize emails by showing different content blocks to different readers — all in ONE send
- No code required, built directly into the editor
- Target by: subscriber attributes, behavior, location, purchase history, subscription tier, custom field values
- Use cases: show upgrade CTA only to free readers; hide "buy this" to people who already bought; show local event invites by geography
- Also works on website pages (not just email)
- Available: Enterprise first, now wider rollout
- Feature page: https://www.beehiiv.com/features/dynamic-content

## Monetization

### Boosts (Earn)
- Get paid when you send subscribers to other newsletters
- CPM (per 1,000 opens) or CPC (per verified click) models
- Feature page: https://www.beehiiv.com/features/boosts-earn

### Ad Network
- ML-matched ad opportunities from premium brands (Nike, Netflix, etc.)
- New opportunities emailed every Sunday
- CPM: earn per 1,000 unique opens
- CPC: earn per verified click
- No ad sales team needed — beehiiv handles matching, assets, and reporting
- All clicks run through fraud/bot verification
- Publishers can preview and approve/reject each opportunity
- Direct Sponsorships: set your own rates, build a storefront, sell directly to brands (separate from Ad Network)
- Feature page: https://www.beehiiv.com/features/ad-network

### Paid Subscriptions
- Gate premium content behind a paywall
- beehiiv takes **0% of earnings** (Stripe fees apply)
- $19M in paid subscription revenue in 2025, up 138% YoY
- Flexible subscription tiers
- Feature page: https://www.beehiiv.com/features/premium-subscriptions

### Digital Products
- Sell ebooks, guides, templates, playbooks, research reports, links (affiliate, membership access)
- Free Products: $0 digital products as lead magnets — no Stripe required, collects subscribers automatically
- Integrates with subscriber profiles, automations, dynamic content, and analytics
- Feature page: https://www.beehiiv.com/features/digital-products

### Discount Codes
- Create and apply discount codes for paid subscriptions and digital products

## Automation

### Automations v4 ⭐ (Latest — March 2026)
This is beehiiv's most powerful automation suite to date. Changelog:
- **A/B testing within automations**: test up to 4 variants in a single automation step
- **Recurring Email Series**: schedule automated newsletters on a set cadence; RSS feed support (Enterprise)
- **Free digital product triggers**: trigger automations when someone claims a free product
- **Bulk Actions**: update, deactivate, or delete multiple automation steps at once
- **More triggers**: expanded behavior-based triggers
- **Improved drag-and-drop editor**: restructure entire journeys easily
- **Enhanced analytics**: full visibility into what's happening across all flows
- Note: Automations are only available on **paid plans**
- Feature page: https://www.beehiiv.com/features/automations
- Product update: https://product.beehiiv.com/p/automations-v4

### Automations v3 (December 2025)
Previous major release (now superseded by v4):
- New triggers: behavior-based, digital product purchase trigger
- Journey Overview dashboard: see how subscribers move through journeys
- Performance Overview: how each email contributes to workflow results
- Subscriber-level insights
- Redesigned automation table with search, filtering, sorting
- Product update: https://product.beehiiv.com/p/automations-v3

### Automation Templates
- Library of proven automation templates (onboarding, re-engagement, upgrade workflows)

### Key Use Cases
- Welcome sequences for new subscribers
- Upgrade prompts (free → paid)
- Re-engagement campaigns for inactive readers
- Digital product delivery and upsell flows
- Behavior-triggered journeys (opened email, clicked link, purchased product)
- Discord/Slack integrations

## Website Builder

### Website Builder v2 (Winter 2025 Release)
- Full no-code website builder
- **AI Website Builder**: describe your site in natural language; AI generates it. Also supports "vibe-code" (describe a mood/aesthetic), image reference, or URL reference for style matching.
- Image Carousels: multiple images in a single element
- Insert From Layers: place elements exactly where you want
- Bulk Select Site Blocks: select all blocks of same type with one click
- Custom Code: add custom HTML/CSS/JS
- Website Templates: pre-built starting points
- YouTube RSS integration: auto-updating video feed from your channel or playlist
- Custom redirects: unlimited, supports wildcard patterns, built-in testing tool
- Custom robots.txt: control how AI crawlers index your content (allow for LLM discovery, or block entirely)
- SEO settings built in
- Feature page: https://www.beehiiv.com/web-builder
- Product updates: https://product.beehiiv.com

### Subscriber Center
- Seamless subscriber management for both publisher and reader
- Readers can manage their own preferences
